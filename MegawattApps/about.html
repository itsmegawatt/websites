Megawatt Apps, LLC. Logo

Megawatt Apps was founded on July 27th 2014 and was originally based in Reston, Virginia. As of August 22nd, 2015, its headquarters have moved to nearby Sterling, Virginia. The company started off as an app development company creating simple but elegant apps such as Beauty Makeup Mirror and To Do Tasks List within its first few months of founding. Closer to the end of the 2014 fiscal year, the company started publishing more complicated apps such as Bitcoin to the Moon and Epic Space Battles.
